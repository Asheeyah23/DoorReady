The problem

One paperwork mistake stalls a family for weeks.

Renters face dense eligibility rules and scattered document requirements with no reliable way to check a packet before submitting — while housing authorities need every eligibility decision to stay in qualified human hands.

Dense eligibility rules

Policy language buried in program guides most renters never see in plain terms.

Scattered requirements

Documents split across income, ID, residency, and household forms with no unified checklist.

No pre-check before submitting

Errors surface only after a caseworker reviews the packet — costing everyone weeks.

The solution

A renter-controlled copilot, not a decision-maker.

DoorReady interprets each document, maps it against the applicable program's rules, and pairs every explanation with a citation back to source policy — flagging gaps in plain language before a human ever has to send it back.

  • Extracts income, ID, residency, and household fields automatically
  • Cites the exact policy behind every rule it explains
  • Compiles a clean packet — the eligibility call stays with the housing authority

Voice accessibility

Every citation, spoken aloud.

Affordable-housing applicants often face literacy barriers, vision impairment, or reading fatigue from dense paperwork. The same cited text DoorReady already generates gets sent to ElevenLabs and played back — a 'Listen' button on any rule explanation or packet summary. No new data model, no separate workflow.
